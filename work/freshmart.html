Freshmart

Web Design and Art Direction

Freshmart, a new online delivery service for fresh foods and produce based in Lima, Perú, looked to us to design their new website and help them flesh out their visual identity. We drew upon the vibrant colours and typography of latin american open-air markets to showcase their service's freshness. This project included homepage design and photography art direction and editing. Development by DF Group in Perú.
