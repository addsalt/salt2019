Sigil & Growler

Website Design & Development.

Vancouver-based Sigil & Growler produces beautifully crafted custom engravings on growlers. They offer a wide array of designs and sizes, as well as allow you to create your own. We expanded on their brand by creating custom illustrations for their different collections. In addition, we developed an expanded colour palette for their brand. The goal was to create a regal, old-world aesthetic that speaks of their craftsmanship. Website soon to launch.
