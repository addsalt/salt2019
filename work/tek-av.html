Tek-AV

Branding, Stationery Design, Web Design & Development.

Newly-established Tek-AV is an Ottawa-based contracting service that specializes in the installation of home entertainment systems. We created an identity that spoke to the owner's love for craftsmanship. The design and typography pay homage to classic audio gear from the 70s and 80s, while using a spot of copper colour and foil pays tribute to the materials of the trade. Our goal was to capture the mood of a home theatre experience. This project included logo design, brand collateral, and website design and development.www.tek-av.com
