Dive App

Branding and App Presentation.

Newly-launched Dive is a video, photo, and text sharing iOS app that automatically captures the reactions of followers. We wanted to capture the fun dynamics of this new form of interaction, creating a versatile and exciting identity. This project included the creation of their logo and typography system, UI elements, application icon, loading animation and other sales collateral.
