The Door

Mural Design and Painting.

Youth engagement centre The Door approached us to design a mural on Ottawa's Bank St. in collaboration with the City of Ottawa, the Chinatown BIA and Paint it Up initiative. The purpose of the mural was to showcase a sense of community, friendship, multiculturalism and diversity. Our asian-artwork-inspired characters, did well in capturing the vibrant sense of community felt in the area, both by youth and adults alike. Soon they were being baptized, and we've been happy to see their debut throughout different social media, as people pick their favorite to pose for a selfie with.
