Rhapsody Strategies

Marketing Collateral, Iconography

Rhapsody Strategies, an Ottawa-based business consulting office, approached us to produce a custom binder for the newest members of the Rhapsody Institute, alongside a series of brochures and banners. The binder is a record of a client's coaching at Rhapsody, and is meant to stay with them through the duration of their partnership. Our designs were well received by clients and stake-holders alike, giving Rhapsody's product the professional, custom presentation they were looking for.
